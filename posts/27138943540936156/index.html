In particular, to gastritis, and then to an ulcer. Helicobacter bacteria aggravate mucosal inflammation. A diet called Table No. 1 is prescribed - sparing nutrition for stomach ulcers and duodenal ulcers. Particular attention in this diet is given to fresh fruits and vegetables. However, they must also be used wisely.

Fruits and vegetables. How to cook

It is important to process all vegetables and fruits with a disease such as an ulcer. A large amount of fiber to the stomach is problematic to process.

It is better to make jelly from quince, since the product is too tart in its raw form. Any jelly, from various fruits, will be an excellent food for a patient with an inflamed gastric mucosa. The benefits of bananas for gastritis and ulcers

Still, what kind of fruit can I eat with a stomach ulcer? The most useful for ulcers are ripe sweet bananas. They are recommended by doctors and with chronic gastritis, and with an ulcer. They have the ability to heal the mucous membrane. Therefore, one banana should be eaten 30 minutes before dinner (but not in the morning on an empty stomach). These tropical fruits contain sodium, phosphorus, magnesium, a lot of potassium. Among the vitamins - B1, B2, B9, vitamin A and C.

Bananas regulate the acid-base balance in the body, help in the work of the intestines, raise the level of the hormone of happiness and improve the functioning of the heart. In addition, bananas improve the functioning of the nervous system. You can eat them fresh and in the form of mashed potatoes. For people with low acidity - this is generally an indispensable product - they need to eat 2-3 fruits a day. But with high acidity - it is better not more than 1 per day. And only after eating.

It will be very good for the stomach if you cook with a blender a banana-berry or banana-mint cocktail.

Apples and pears for ulcers

No less healthy than bananas are our regular apples. The fiber they contain is called pectin. Such fiber does not harm the stomach. And as soon as the aggravation passes, you can and should immediately begin to eat apples, mashed as mashed potatoes at the beginning. And then, if the remission lasts a long time, eat whole apples.

Apples are eaten with carrots and sour cream. You can make fruit drinks, jelly and bake in the oven. But there is one rule. It is better to eat apples of sweet varieties raw. Sour green apples will still injure the mucous membrane with their irritating effect.

Apples are especially needed for those with bleeding ulcers. Who hemoglobin is lost with blood. After all, as you know, apples more than other fruits raise the level of hemoglobin. But it is not necessary to improve blood with pomegranate, since pomegranate contains a lot of vitamin C.

Pears are also completely safe for the stomach. They also have a huge amount of vitamins: there is vitamin A, PP (nicotinic acid), K, and riboflavin - vitamin B2. This is a great way to get the necessary complex of vitamins and minerals without fear of complications. However, with dried pears, you need to be careful and monitor your health.

Olives in the diet

What are olives useful for? Olives have a beneficial effect on the functioning of the entire system - on the intestines, stomach, pancreas. Olive oil is recommended every day to drink 1 tbsp. spoon before breakfast, lunch and dinner. About 20-30 minutes before meals.

This product, like apples, contains pectin. This substance removes toxins from the body.

Are dried fruits allowed

Dried fruits are in many ways dangerous for ulcer patients. When you adhere to a strict diet, do not risk deviating from the dietary rules that the gastroenterologist gave you.

But during the period of remission, you can eat a couple of dried slices of your favorite fruit. From a very small amount of dried apricots there will be no harm. As well as from several grapes, your ulcer will not grow instantly in size. If you really like, for example, dried apricots, raisins, dried figs, then twice a month (if your stomach does not hurt) you can afford to feast on yourself. In any case, it is much more useful than cigarettes or sweet rolls. Dried fruits retain useful elements, unlike those fruits that have undergone heat treatment.

It is recommended to make dried fruit compotes. Their benefits can hardly be overestimated for patients with low acidity. But to overdo it with the consumption of compotes or jelly from dried fruits is also not necessary.
