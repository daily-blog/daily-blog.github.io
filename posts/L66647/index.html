Potato starch for the face: mask recipes, effectiveness and reviews

Currently, homemade care products are widely used. After all, they have a completely natural composition. They are safe, effective, and with regular use can heal and rejuvenate the skin. How to use potato starch for the face? The article will discuss methods of using the product, recipes for masks and their effectiveness.

The composition and properties of starch

Potato starch for the face has a positive effect on the skin, thanks to a variety of nutrients. After all, it consists of:

  1. Vitamin C. It promotes the return to life of the cellular structure of the skin. Starch reliably protects it from destruction and promotes recovery.
  2. Niacin. Improves lipid metabolism and activates the growth of new cells, making them viable.
  3. Choline. The substance regulates fat metabolism and normalizes the process of sebum secretion.
  4. Potassium. The component helps to moisturize the skin and helps it retain moisture in its structure.
  5. Iron. It improves blood circulation and oxygen saturation of the blood.
  6. Carbohydrates. They give skin cells energy.

The starch contains vitamins that improve the functioning of all tissue structures. These are thiamine, riboflavin and others.

What will give starch to the skin?

The mask from this product is suitable for all types of skin, even sensitive. The provided action is gentle, delicate and restoring. The face benefits of potato starch are as follows:

  • the product will help the young skin integrate to return a radiant appearance, eliminating traces of fatigue;
  • on a dry epidermis, peeling, redness and inflammation will decrease, the feeling of tightness completely disappears;
  • hypersensitive skin will reduce its susceptibility, significantly increase protective functions, starch gently cares for it;
  • a fatty dermis will improve its appearance, due to the normalization of the sebaceous glands, the pores are tightened and well cleansed, the skin turns into matte.

A face mask made of potato starch smoothes small wrinkles on the skin, significantly slows down age-related changes and whitens it. Most often it is used for aging facial skin, on which the positive qualities of the product are manifested as much as possible.

The benefits of potato starch for facial skin are as follows: masks will transform the skin within a month. Many women claim that the drug has the effect of Botox. The skin is tightened and there is no negative effect on it.

For the face, you can use not only potato, but also corn starch. The first remedy has a thicker consistency, so its anti-aging properties are higher. For young girls, corn starch is suitable, and for older women, potato.

If there are no contraindications for the use of the product, it can be constantly used to improve the condition of the skin.

How to make masks

The tool can be used for any type of skin, given a number of nuances. A mask of potato starch for the face from wrinkles and other problems of the dermis has certain features.

  1. It is first necessary to prepare the skin for the procedure. Use a foam or tonic to remove makeup. Rinse face with water.
  2. With a sponge, brush or fingertips, the product is applied to the skin along massage lines. The use of the mask begins from the bottom up. Initially, the product is applied to the chin area, from the corners of the lips to the ears. The mask is applied from the middle of the forehead to the hairline and temples. Additionally applied to the neck and decollete. The area around the eyes remains clean.
  3. The mask should be evenly distributed over the surface of the skin.
  4. Within 20-25 minutes, it is not recommended to move the facial muscles. You should not touch the mask with your hands either. It is best to lie down and relax.
  5. The product is removed from the face with a soft sponge dipped in water. Then it is washed without soap. Sometimes instead of water, herbal decoctions or milk are used.

At the very end of the procedure, apply a nourishing cream to the face to soothe and moisturize the skin.

In the process of preparing the mask, the following rule must be observed: dilute starch only with warm liquid. It can be herbal infusion, milk and more. If this condition is not taken into account, then clumps will be present in the mask and it will lie badly on the face. An even worse effect will occur if the starch is diluted with boiling water. In this case, an adhesive will be obtained that will only harm the skin.

If potato starch is not at hand, you can also use corn starch. It is also rich in nutrients that positively affect the dermis. For masks, only quality ingredients must be used.

When choosing the components of the mask, be sure to consider their tolerance to the skin. Before the procedure, you must conduct a test. Apply a small amount to the wrist. If redness and other allergic reactions do not appear, then the mask is allowed to be used without fear.

How to smooth skin

According to reviews, potato starch for facial wrinkles is used in combination with various components.

To prepare the mask used starch (30 g), 1 tbsp. a spoonful of sour cream and 80 g of carrot juice.

The preparation process does not take much time. Starch is dissolved in 100 ml of water. Then the resulting mass is gradually poured into boiling water (500 ml). On a small fire, cook the mixture until thickened. The result should be a mass that, in consistency, resembles a thick cream. Cool and add other components. To stir thoroughly. The mask is applied to the skin with a thin layer. If there are a large number of wrinkles on the face, then a second layer can be used. This should be done quickly so that the previous one does not have time to dry.

The tool is allowed to be used every day. The positive effect becomes noticeable after 4 days of continuous use.

A mask with the effect of Botox can be on the face for no more than 25 minutes. The remaining product must be stored in a cool place for 3 days. This should be done as a last resort, because then the mask loses many useful substances.

To get rid of wrinkles, a face mask with potato starch is prepared using several components. One tablespoon of the product is combined with milk. Add to the mixture a teaspoon of peach or olive oil. Keep on face for 15 minutes. Wash off with water.

Wrinkled potato starch for the face can be used to restore the dermis.

The main components of the mask include: 1 teaspoon of starch, egg yolk, 1 teaspoon of olive oil and a little tomato pulp.

Before preparing the mask, pour tomato over boiling water, peel it. Then, the remaining components are added to its pulp. The resulting product is applied to the face with a thin layer. Hold the mask for 10 minutes. Then the product is washed off with warm water.

To restore the life of withered skin, the following remedy is prepared. The main components include: 1 tbsp. a spoonful of starch, 20 ml cream and a small banana.

The fruit is thoroughly kneading and the remaining components are added. The result is a viscous mass. It is applied to the skin in several stages. When the first layer dries, a second and then a third is applied.

The mask is kept on the face for 30 minutes. The effect of its use appears after 2 weeks and lasts 3 months. The procedure must be done once a week for 60 days.

  3. For normal skin. For a mask, mix Art. teaspoon of starch and 1 teaspoon of salt. Mix the components and dilute with warm milk to a paste-like state.

Masks must be carried out every other day, the full course is designed for 1 month. The exposure time is - 30 minutes.

They are thoroughly mixed until a homogeneous composition is obtained. The product is applied to the face and held for 15-20 minutes. Overexposing such a tool on the face is not recommended.

As a bleaching agent use a mixture that consists of 1 tbsp. tablespoons of starch and the same amount of lemon juice. The positive effect will become noticeable after the first procedure, and age spots will lighten. Sometimes, 1-2 drops of tea tree oil are used to enhance the positive effect.

To rid the skin of yellowness, use egg white and 1 tbsp. a spoonful of kefir and starch. Such a tool is applied to the face with a thick layer. Stand for 15 minutes and rinse with warm water.

Contraindications

Potato starch for the face may not be used in all cases; there are some restrictions on its use. This is extremely rare, because an allergy to the drug is extremely rare.

Masks should not be applied to the skin during serious inflammatory processes. And it can be cuts or open wounds, as well as herpes. If there are no inflammations on the face, then you can safely apply potato starch to your skin. The procedure should be carried out no more than 2-3 times a week.

An excellent effect can be detected after 10-12 sessions, although much depends on the individual characteristics of the skin.
