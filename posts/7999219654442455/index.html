Health hazard

Like all food products, pork skin contains certain disadvantages that can significantly harm health. First of all, we are talking about fats. Each piece of the product in question may contain 9 grams of this element. In that case, if a person has a diet within which he should not exceed 2000 calories per day, then the individual will be able to afford only a maximum of 78 grams. This is equal to 700 calories. It should be noted that 30 grams of pork skin takes from twelve to twenty percent fat.

Cholesterol is another harmful ingredient that should be avoided. Pork skin is filled with saturated unhealthy fats and cholesterol. In the process of mixing these substances, low density lipoprotein increases (we are talking about bad cholesterol). In the case of an increase in its level, the arteries are blocked, which can lead to a heart attack. 30 grams of pork skin contains more than 3 grams of saturated fatty elements and 27 milligrams of cholesterol.

Some people are not even aware of the dangers of pork skins.

Sodium-rich pork skin can have a bad effect on the heart, while increasing blood pressure in humans. Manufacturers significantly degrade the quality of pork by adding flavor enhancers to it, which in general destabilizes overall health. Usually people consume 2.3 mg of sodium per day. But a person with diseases of the heart system is allowed to consume no more than 1.5 mg per day.
